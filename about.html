Our Story

From humble beginnings to becoming a leading printing and design studio

Who We Are

PrintCraft Studio is a passionate team of designers, printers, and creative professionals dedicated to bringing your visions to life through exceptional print and design services.

Founded in 2015, we've grown from a small print shop to a full-service creative studio serving businesses and individuals across Nigeria and beyond.

Our mission is to combine cutting-edge technology with artisanal craftsmanship to deliver print products that exceed expectations.

Our Journey

2015 - Humble Beginnings

Founded as a small print shop with just two employees and a single printing press in Lagos.

2017 - Expansion

Added graphic design services and moved to a larger facility to accommodate growing demand.

2019 - Technology Upgrade

Invested in state-of-the-art digital printing equipment and expanded our service offerings.

2021 - Brand Evolution

Rebranded as PrintCraft Studio to reflect our expanded creative services and premium positioning.

2023 - Present Day

Now serving clients across Africa with a team of 20+ professionals and an ever-expanding range of services.
